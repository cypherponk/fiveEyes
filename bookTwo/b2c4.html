A Compilation

One of the biggest reasons for creating this site was to bring a whole lot of information together. I'll admit that there's a plethora of available tutorials, videos, and even podcasts out there that have been great resources for myself and for others in the infosec community.

Here, I wanted to collect a list of all the best resources I've found so far. What I'm including is for all levels of understanding from the unexperienced n00b to the employed professional.

Blogs & Magazines

GeeksforGeeks [link]

Their description: "A Computer Science portal for geeks. It contains well written, well thought and well explained computer science and programming articles, quizzes and practice/competitive programming/company interview Questions."

CSOnline [link]

Their description: "CSO serves enterprise security decision-makers and users with the critical information they need to stay ahead of evolving threats and defend against criminal cyberattacks. With incisive content that addresses all security disciplines from risk management to network defense to fraud and data loss prevention, CSO offers unparalleled depth and insight to support key decisions and investments for IT security professionals."

edureka! [link]

Their description: "Edureka is an online training provider with the most effective learning system in the world. We help professionals learn trending technologies for career growth."

CISO Mag [link]

Their description: "Infosecurity Magazine is the award winning online magazine dedicated to the strategy, insight and technology of information security."

Dark Reading [link]

Their description: "Cyber security's comprehensive news site is now an online community for security professionals, outlining cyber threats and the technologies for defending against them."

YouTube Channels

Security & Compliance Weekly

Weekly videos with a focus in information security.

SecureNinjaTV

SecureNinjaTV covers a wide range of Cybersecurity industry conferences, events and trade shows each year.

Null Byte

Great channel for tutorials with easy-to-follow instructions.

Computerphile

An interesting format of informational storytelling.

Podcasts

WIRED Tech in Two

A great podcast for general updates into the tech community. Sometimes they'll cover data breaches or highlight stories in the infosec community. Easy to follow. Great for beginners.

Darknet Diaries

Awesome content by an awesome interviewer. Engaging stories with a focus on cyber crime. Great for any level.
